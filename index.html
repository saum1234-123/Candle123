Our Story

Founded in 2018, Gamya Candles began as a passion project in a small home studio. What started as a creative outlet quickly grew into a mission to create beautiful, sustainable candles that elevate everyday moments.

Each Gamya candle is hand-poured in our workshop using only the finest ingredients. We source premium soy wax, lead-free cotton wicks, and carefully curated fragrance oils to ensure exceptional quality and performance.

Sustainability

Our reusable vessels and eco-conscious packaging minimize environmental impact.

Craftsmanship

Every candle receives personal attention through every step of the process.
